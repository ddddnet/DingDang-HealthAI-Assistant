2. Service Usage Rules

2.1 Check-in Features

Users can check in using the following methods:

  • Upload food photos (e.g., breakfast, lunch): Users can take photos of their meals and upload them to the platform after each meal.
  • Record exercise data (e.g., running, fitness): Users can connect smart devices (e.g., fitness trackers, smart watches) or manually input their exercise data.
  • Upload weight photos: Users can regularly upload weight photos to track weight changes.

2.2 AI Analysis

AI analysis features include:

  • Food nutrition identification: The system uses AI to identify food types and nutritional components from uploaded photos.
  • Exercise effect evaluation: Based on recorded exercise data, AI evaluates exercise effectiveness including calories burned and intensity.
  • Weight trend prediction: Combining weight photos with other data, AI predicts weight change trends.

Note: AI analysis results are for reference only and should not be used as medical diagnosis. While our AI analysis provides health suggestions and data references, it cannot replace professional medical diagnosis and advice. Users with health concerns should consult a doctor or health professional.

二、服务使用规则

2.1 打卡功能

用户可使用以下方式打卡：

  • 上传食物照片（如早餐、午餐）：用户可以在每天用餐后，拍摄食物照片并上传至平台。
  • 记录运动数据（如跑步、健身）：用户可以通过连接智能设备（如运动手环、智能手表等）或手动输入的方式，记录自己的运动数据。
  • 上传体重照片：用户可以定期上传体重照片，用于记录体重变化。

2.2 AI分析

AI分析功能包括：

  • 食物营养成分识别：系统将根据用户上传的食物照片，利用AI技术识别食物的种类和营养成分。
  • 运动效果评估：根据用户记录的运动数据，AI将评估用户的运动效果，包括消耗的卡路里、运动强度等。
  • 体重变化趋势预测：结合用户上传的体重照片和其他相关数据，AI将预测用户的体重变化趋势。

注意：AI分析结果仅供参考，不作为医疗诊断依据。虽然我们的AI分析功能能够提供一定的健康建议和数据参考，但它不能替代专业的医疗诊断和建议。如果用户有任何健康问题或疑虑，应及时咨询医生或专业的健康顾问。
